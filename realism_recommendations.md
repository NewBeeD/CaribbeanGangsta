# Realism Recommendations for Caribbean Gangsta — Rise of the Drug Lord

**Compiled from GDD Analysis & Real-World Research**  
**Date:** 2026-07-07  
**Basis:** Original docs + UNODC, DEA, InSight Crime, trafficking studies. Focus: Caribbean transit ops (cocaine-dominant, corruption, US arms, adaptive networks). Preserves engagement model, ethical guardrails, idle design, story generator.

## 1. Supply Chain & Operations

- **Structure:** Farms/labs (Caribbean cannabis, precursor imports) → smuggling (go-fast boats, semi-submersibles, drones, containers, air drops) → distribution (US/Europe via Caribbean hubs).
- **Diversification:** Cocaine primary; add synthetics (pills/meth/fentanyl precursors from Asia), arms, side revenues (extortion later).
- **Dynamic Elements:** Markets fluctuate on supply gluts, weather, LE ops, rivals. Chaos Engine forces route shifts (e.g., to Guyana/Suriname after disruptions).
- **Narrative Integration:** Tie beats to thresholds (first international route → DEA escalation; arms unlock as paradigm shift).



## 2. Prices & Economy Math (Update doc 01)

**Base Prices (per kg; ranges for volatility):**

- Weed: Buy $700–1500, Sell $1200–2,500. Low heat, high volume.
- Synthetics: Buy $2k–5k (precursors/finished), Sell $10k–30k+.
- Cocaine: Buy $1.5k–3k (source), mid $5k–10k, Sell $20k–60k+ (wholesale). Gram street $60–200.
- Arms (crate/rifle): Handguns $500–2k; rifles $1k–5k+ (US Iron River).
- **Mechanics:** 20–50% volatility (visible trends). Higher margins with risk/heat. Laundering sinks (trade-based, Black Market Peso Exchange, crypto).

**Idle/Laundering:** Fronts (resorts, casinos, real estate, music, political donations) at realistic rates. Offline accrual with decisions on return.

**Legacy:** Heir inherits partial networks/routes.

## 3. Storage & Corruption (Enhance doc 09)

- **Storage:** Diversify across floor/jungle/safehouse/port containers. One raid = one location (anti-wipe). Guards tie to crew betrayal. Decoys for clever play.
- **Bribes:** Port officials 5–20%+ shipment value (variable, negotiable). Factors: heat, rivals, greed. Show price + odds.
- **Payroll:** Cops, DEA insiders, customs, politicians, judges. Deterministic benefits + telegraphed betrayal ("plata o plomo"). Ties to relatedness.
- **Progression:** Mid-game unlock via progressive disclosure.



## 4. Crew & Relatedness (doc 02)

- **Depth:** Memory logs for specific events/favors. Agendas drive emergent conflict. Family layer for high-stakes relatedness.
- **Betrayal:** Readable arcs (warnings → intervention → flip/snitch). Recruitment from community/poaching.

## 5. Arms Dealing & Rivals

- **Arms:** US-sourced (straw purchases). Synergies with protection/turf. Higher heat.
- **Rivals:** Distinct styles (violent, political, logistics). Grudges over routes/ports. Temporary alliances.



## 6. Debt & Loan Sharks (doc 10)

- **Tiers:** Street (high interest) → cartel financiers. Collateral limited. Interest in-game only (freeze offline).
- **Escalation:** Readable ladder (vig → visits → seizure → rivalry). Agency via payment/favors/violence.
- **Ethics:** Opt-in, recoverable, no real-life guilt.



## 7. Heat, LE & Chaos Engine

- **Tiers:** Local → DEA → CIA/Interpol. Tip-offs, raids (one location), counter-intel.
- **Events:** Hurricanes, supply crashes, LE surges. Bounded variable rewards.



## 8. Playstyles & Autonomy (GDD §10)

- Violent (arms/intimidation), Business (laundering/negotiation), Political (corruption/influence), Low-Profile/Tech (stealth/crypto).
- Reputation tracks affect prices, alliances, beats.



## 9. Monetization & Live Ops (doc 03)
